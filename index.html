About Me

Experienced Communication Expert with a demonstrated history of working in the media and communications industries in the last 26 years.
Skilled in News Writing, Editing, Feature Writing, News Production, New Media, Social Media and Journalism.
Strong Media and Communication Professional, PR, Media Relations, Brand Management etc.
Creative and innovative problem-solver exercises key strengths in workflow optimization, process re-engineering, and strategic scheduling techniques.
Offers track record of delivering superior-quality results for industry-leading publication conglomerates.
Hold Diploma in Journalism at the foremost Nigerian Institute of Journalism (NIJ) in Ikeja, Lagos and a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) focused in Philosophy & Public Affairs from University of Ibadan, Ibadan, Oyo State in Nigeria.
As an editor and vice chairman of the National Sports Writers Association, I focus on delivering insightful analysis and thought-provoking editorials that inspire sports lovers worldwide.
